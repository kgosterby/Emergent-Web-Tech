Jon's Best Burgers
Double ShackBurger @ Shake Shack

A little bit expensive for a diner feel, but the meat is high quality, and I don't have to have a waiter hovering over me. Just let me eat my perfectly thin and crispy, slightly overpriced but delicious burger in peace.

Get me there!
Double cheeseburger @ Au Cheval

Probably the most gourmet greasy burger I've had. Excellent quality, priced for the neighborhood (meaning about $2 too much). Biggest complaint here is the neighborhood. Don't go on a weekend night.

Get me there!
Brixton Burger @ The Brixton

The fanciest burger on the list. $13 for a relatively small burger seems costly, and it is, but man, that is a tasty sandwich.

Get me there!
Double cheeseburger @ Red Hot Ranch

I think this burger is less than $5. Very comparable to In-N-Out - greasy, sloppy, and delicious. Also, they're open late, and there's never a line.

Get me there!
